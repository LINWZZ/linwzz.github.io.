How to convert your files into MP3

Let's start a project!The following are the steps for you to transcode your file into MP3.

  1. Click Audio and choose MP3.

  2. Click Add file.

  3. Click OK.

  4. Click the file.

  5. Click Start.

  6. Completed.

  7. Right click and Open Output Folder and you can find the MP3 file.

    8. If you want to convert to other audio format, the steps are the same as converting to MP3. If you want to convert to MP4, then the first step is to click Video, and the rest of the steps are the same.

    That's it. Good luck.
