How to clip video with Format Facotry

    Let's start a project! The following are the steps for you to clip your video.

  1. Click Video to select a video format. Usually we choose MP4.

  2. Click Add file.

  3. Click Clip.

    4. The range of time now is at zero.

  4. Set the range of time in line with the start time and end time of the video you want to capture.For example, let's set the start time at 2 minute and end time at 5 minute.

  5. Click OK.

  6. Then the captured video is on the task list.Click OK

  7. Click the file.

  8. Click Start.

  9. Completed.

  10. Right click and Open Output Folder.

    11. If you want to clip your audio, the steps are the same as clipping your video except that the first setp is to click Audio and choose MP3.

    Have a try. Good luck.
